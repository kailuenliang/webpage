Kai Luen is a Chinese-American sound artist, music producer/ composer currently based between Beijing and Los Angeles.

His music has been released by SBKVLT, Ran Music, BBE records, and Modern Sky. He is part of Beijing experimental music/ art group Wan Who? with Zhao Xia Xia and Mr. Asbo. He also has produced and released music as [Soulspeak] and [Silicone Moons].
